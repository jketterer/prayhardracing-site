"Keeping kids on the track, and off the street."

Welcome to the official site of Pray Hard Racing! We are a team of drag racers that aims to teach kids how to race. Based in PA, we run most weekends at Maple Grove Raceway, but also make trips down to Cecil County Raceway, Numidia Dragway, and Island Dragway. In July, you'll find us down in Bristol, TN at the JDRL Eastern Conference Finals.
